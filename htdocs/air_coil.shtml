Wcalc Air Core Solenoid Model

Notes Regarding the Calculations

The primary reference used for these calculations is

  1. Geffe, Philip R. "The Design of Single-Layer Solenoids for RF Filters," Microwave Journal, Vol 39, No 12, December 1996, pp. 70-76

Note: There are a few errors in the original Geffe article. Corrections for some of the errors (typographical in nature) appeared in the Microwave Journal, but not all of them. I have corrected them here.

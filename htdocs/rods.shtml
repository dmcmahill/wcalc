Comments

The development in the Aebischer paper is the primary reference. Aebischer provides some accuracy comparisons between his expressions and exact numerical solutions and also compares to other common formula. The Aebischer results are quite good. In the case for relatively short wires, the Aebischer results are much more accurate than the other common expressions.

Currently this module requires equal diameter and length wires with no axial offset. Hopefully this restriction can be removed at some point. In addition, frequency dependence is not accounted for. I believe there is some later work from Aebischer that may show the path towards including frequency dependence accurately.

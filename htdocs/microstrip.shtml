Comments

The primary reference for characteristic impedance is the Hammerstad and Jensen paper. The conductor losses are found using Wheeler's incremental inductance approach. Those results are then modified to account for surface roughness. Note that the equation for surface roughness given in Fooks and Zakarevicius contains a typographical error. The correct equation may be found in either the Hammerstad and Jensen paper or the Hammerstad and Bekkadal report.

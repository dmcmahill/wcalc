CGI-Wcalc

The CGI frontend to Wcalc provides an easy way for the CAD support staff in a company to make wcalc available to engineers who may be working on several different computing platforms without needing to compile Wcalc for each system. The CGI frontend provides full analysis and synthesis capability.

  • Air Core Inductors (Single Layer Solenoid)
  • Parallel Rectangular Bar Inductance
  • Coaxial Transmission Line
  • Coplanar Waveguide
  • Integrated Circuit Microstrip (MIS)
  • Printed Circuit Coupled Microstrip
  • Coupled Stripline
  • Printed Circuit Microstrip (MIM)
  • Series/Parallel RC Network
  • Printed Circuit Symmetric Stripline (MIMIM)

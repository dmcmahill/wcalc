Tgif - WWW Hypertext Home Page
What is tgif?
  • Tgif (pronounced t-g-i-f) is an Xlib based interactive 2-D drawing tool under X11 on Linux and most UNIX platforms.
  • Tgif becomes a hyper-structured-graphics (or hyper-graphics) editor/browser on the World-Wide-Web in version 2.16 (released on Dec 3, 1994).

    Do: 

        tgif http://bourbon.cs.umd.edu:8001/tgif/index.obj
    to see the tgif hyper-graphics home page.
